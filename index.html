CERTIFICATION PROCESS

We work for you !

Application

You submit an initial application fee and site list describing your company products. This information helps EKO-GUARANTEE establish a quote. Send us a signed contract and quote along with your risk assessment fee payment to get started.

Document Submission

You submit detailed information about the processes an products for review. This includes risk assessment,SOPs, production facilities and co packers information.

Document Review

EKO_GUARANTEE technical evaluator reviews the documents you have submitted. We may ask for clarification or more information during the reiews

On-Site Inspection Ment Review

EKO-GUARANTEE will need to schedule an on-site inspection of your facilities. This is determined by review of your risk assessment.

Corrective Action Review And Certification Decision

Any required Corrective action respones eill be reviewed for compliance and a certification decision is made.

Certification And Ongoing Compliance

After approval, eligible to use your Non-GMO mark. An annual renewal evaluation will take place from this point forward. Products and facilities can be added to scope mid year.
